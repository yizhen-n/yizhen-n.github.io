Ideas

This is a playground to explore future interaciton concepts following the route of:
Technology → Interaction possibility → Benefits in life

A chatbot is invited to join group chat

People are now getting used to ask chat bot general questions personally. But if these questions need group discussion, can we introduce a bot into the group chat? For instance, when pals look for a hang out plan, picking activities can be quite aimless. In this case, a bot can be called out to offer initial options to enable further effective discussion.

What if OCR/ASR can help teams boost creativity

OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and ASR(Automatic Speech Recognition) have largely improved our efficiency of communication and reduced boring documentation. But if these smart systems use the seen/heard information to search for more informations, the user's creativity can grow more profoundly.

Imagine a seminar with papers found from related topic, a product development meeting with inspirations collected in the team's cloud folder, or a brainstorm session with images feed.

What if handicrafts can store digital stories?

Every hand crafted batik fabric has unique crack patterns. If these patterns are saved as an ID, the augmented reality technology can identify it. Then the digital layer and beautiful handicrafts together compose a touchable warmth.
